About Us

Sometime you just need CAKE

Welcome to Cake Bakery, your go-to destination for delightful cakes and pastries for all occasions! My name is Vaishali Kharva, and I am the proud owner and head baker here at Cake Bakery. With a passion for baking and a commitment to quality, I founded this bakery to bring joy to your celebrations through delicious, handcrafted treats.

At Cake Bakery, we believe that every cake tells a story. Whether it's a birthday, wedding, anniversary, or any special event, we're dedicated to creating custom cakes that reflect your unique style and taste. From elegant wedding cakes to whimsical birthday creations, we work closely with our clients to bring their visions to life.

Our talented team of bakers and decorators takes pride in using only the finest ingredients to ensure that every bite is a taste of perfection. From classic vanilla and chocolate to exotic flavors and intricate designs, we offer a wide variety of options to satisfy your sweet cravings.

Thank you for choosing Cake Bakery to be a part of your memorable moments. We look forward to serving you and creating delicious memories together!
